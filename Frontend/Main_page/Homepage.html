Buckeye Farm

About Us

Buckeye Farm is an online store that provide produce to people in need. We take produce deemed "unfit" by grocery stores but are actually in good condition, and sell it online. This helps with reducing food waste as well as use it in a sustainable way.

Our overall mission is reduce food waste and provide another option to get food. Once you order on our website, we will ship the food to your address between 2-3 days or a week, depending on your location.
